Look at the first digits of the sample

signifd(X)
##  [1] 6 1 5 2 7 5 7 4 3 1 2 2 1 5 6 2 2 3 1 3

Perform a Chi-squared Test on the samples first digits using defaults

chisq.benftest(X)
